Welcome to Women In Engineering Society Student Chapter
Ahmedabad University Students' Branch of IEEE

The Women In Engineering society student chapter of Ahmedabad University Students' branch of IEEE fosters the development and facilitates the exchange of scientific and technological knowledge in Robotics and Automation that benefits members, the profession and humanity.

The Women In Engineering society student chapter of Ahmedabad University Students' branch of IEEE desires to be the most recognized and respected global organization in Robotics and Automation.
