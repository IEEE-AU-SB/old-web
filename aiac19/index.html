One Day Virtual National Workshop on
AI for fight Against COVID19 (AIAC19)

24 October, 2020

Registration Deadline: 22 October, 2020

AIAC19


In this worldwide pandemic situation, it is essential to look for newer technology to control the Corona Virus spread. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is one such potent defense against this virus. It helps prevent the infection in real-time or predict the mortality rate by analyzing data. It is also useful in population screening, medical planning, identifying hotspots, and bodies with appropriate evidence and advice on infection control. The virtual national workshop AIAC19 aims to bring forward such AI applications, which can help fight menace.


Objectives

  • To make the audience aware about the recent advances in artificial intelligence and their effectiveness in fighting COVID-19.
  • To make the audience ponder on the questions for which we need satisfactory answers in coming times.
  • To showcase future possibilities of the healthcare and industrial revolutions that could serve as a better place for everyone.
  • To organize a seminar with an impactful footprint in the community at large.
