Question and Hints for interactive sessions
  • You will be grouped with other participants in a group according to the preferences you have provided in the registration form.
  • Your group and some other groups will be instructed to join in one of the three breakout rooms of the virtual session by Zoom.
  • In the breakout room, although you are in a group, you can individually participate in concept mapping activity. Visit the following link for the tutorial on how to use the tool: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PFm3KGGqfsk
  • The activity in each breakout room would be moderated by a respective faculty member from Ahmedabad University.
  • After completion of the activities, there will be break of 15 minutes.
  • The best concept mapping group from a breakout room will be declared after the break and then they would present their concept map to all the participants.
    (Each group member with the best concept mapping would receive an attractive price)
